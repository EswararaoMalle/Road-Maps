DSA Roadmap

Learn Basic Data Structures( arrays, linked lists, stacks, queues, and hash maps.)
Understand Complexity Analysis( time and space complexity, Big O notation, and how to optimize algorithms.)
Master Sorting Algorithms(quicksort, mergesort, and heapsort.)
Practice Searching Algorithms(binary search, depth-first search (DFS), and breadth-first search (BFS).)
Learn Recursion and Backtracking
Study Advanced Data Structures( trees, graphs, heaps, and tries.)
Practice with Competitive Programming( LeetCode, Codeforces, and HackerRank.)
